Email Sequence Information

Each sector can have up to 4 email templates that are sent automatically:

  • Email 1: Initial outreach (sent immediately when company is added)
  • Email 2: First follow-up (sent 4 days after Email 1)
  • Email 3: Second follow-up (sent 5 days after Email 2)
  • Email 4: Final follow-up (sent 10 days after Email 3)

Emails are only sent on Tuesday, Wednesday, and Thursday.
