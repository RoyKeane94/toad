Privacy Policy

Your privacy is important to us. This policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information.

Last updated: December 2024

Table of Contents

1. Information We Collect 2. How We Use Your Information 3. Information Sharing 4. Data Security 5. Your Rights 6. Cookies and Tracking 7. Data Retention 8. Contact Us

1. Information We Collect

Personal Information

When you create an account with Toad, we collect:

  • • Name (first and last)
  • • Email address
  • • Password (encrypted)

Usage Information

We automatically collect information about how you use Toad:

  • • Task and grid data you create
  • • Login times and frequency
  • • Browser type and version
  • • IP address and location (general)

2. How We Use Your Information

We use your information to:

  • Provide and maintain the Toad service
  • Authenticate your account and prevent fraud
  • Improve our service and develop new features
  • Respond to your support requests
  • Send important service notifications

We do not sell, rent, or share your personal information with third parties for marketing purposes.

3. Information Sharing

We may share your information only in the following circumstances:

  • Legal Requirements: When required by law or to protect our rights
  • Service Providers: With trusted partners who help us operate Toad (they cannot use your data for other purposes)
  • Business Transfers: In the event of a merger or acquisition (with prior notice)
  • With Your Consent: When you explicitly agree to share information

4. Data Security

We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your information:

Encryption

All data is encrypted in transit and at rest using industry-standard protocols.

Secure Hosting

Our servers are hosted in secure, certified data centers with 24/7 monitoring.

Access Controls

Strict access controls ensure only authorized personnel can access your data.

Regular Backups

Your data is regularly backed up to prevent loss and ensure availability.

5. Your Rights

You have the following rights regarding your personal information:

Access

Request a copy of your personal information

Correction

Update or correct inaccurate information

Deletion

Request deletion of your account and data

Portability

Export your data in a portable format

To exercise these rights, please contact us at privacy@toad.com

6. Cookies and Tracking

We use cookies and similar technologies to:

  • Keep you signed in to your account
  • Remember your preferences and settings
  • Analyze how you use Toad to improve our service
  • Protect against fraud and abuse

You can control cookies through your browser settings. However, disabling cookies may affect your ability to use some features of Toad.

7. Data Retention

We retain your information for as long as necessary to:

  • Provide you with Toad services
  • Comply with legal obligations
  • Resolve disputes and prevent fraud

When you delete your account, we will delete your personal information within 30 days, except where we are required to retain it by law.

8. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy or how we handle your information, please contact us:

Email: privacy@toad.com
Contact Form: Contact Us

This Privacy Policy was last updated on December 2024. We will notify you of any material changes by posting the updated policy on this page.
