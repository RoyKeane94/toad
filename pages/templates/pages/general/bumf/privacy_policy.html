Privacy Policy

We believe in simplicity and transparency. Here's how we handle your data.

Last updated: July 2025

The Short Version

Your privacy is incredibly important to us. We are a "no-frills" tool, and that philosophy extends to your data.

  • We only collect the data we need to make Toad work.
  • We will never sell your data to third parties.
  • We use a privacy-focused analytics tool that does not use cookies and does not track you across the web.
  • You are in complete control of your data.

Table of Contents

1. Information We Collect 2. How We Use Your Information 3. Who We Share Data With (Our Service Providers) 4. Our Approach to Cookies 5. Your Rights & Data Control 6. Contact Us

1. Information We Collect

Information you provide to us:

When you sign up for Toad, you voluntarily give us certain information:

  • Your email address and a securely encrypted password to create your account.
  • The content you create within the app, such as the names of your grids and the text of your tasks.

Information we collect automatically:

To improve our service, we use a privacy-focused analytics tool called Plausible. It helps us understand how people are using Toad in general, but it does so anonymously and without using cookies. We do not track your individual behaviour. The kind of aggregate data we look at includes:

  • Which pages are most popular (e.g., the template gallery).
  • How many users create a second grid.
  • The general country of origin of our visitors.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use your information for one reason: to provide you with a great, simple tool.

  • To operate the Toad service and let you log in securely.
  • To respond to you if you email us for help (at tom@meettoad.co.uk).
  • To understand our anonymous usage data to make the product better.

3. Who We Share Data With (Our Service Providers)

As a solo maker, I rely on a few trusted, best-in-class services to run Toad. We only share the data they need to function. We will never sell your data.

Railway

Our web hosting provider. Your encrypted grid and task data is stored on their secure servers.

Stripe

Our payment processor. If you upgrade to a paid plan, your payment information is sent directly to Stripe. We never see or store your credit card details.

Plausible Analytics

Our privacy-focused analytics provider. They collect anonymous, aggregate data and do not use cookies.

4. Our Approach to Cookies

We do not use cookies for analytics, advertising, or tracking. The only cookies we use are strictly necessary for the app to work:

Session Cookie (sessionid)

Remembers that you are logged in as you navigate the site.

Security Cookie (csrftoken)

Protects you and the site from common security vulnerabilities.

Because these are essential for functionality, we don't need to show you an annoying cookie banner.

5. Your Rights & Data Control

You are always in control of your data. You have the right to:

  • Access and update your account information at any time.
  • Delete your account permanently. When you do, all of your grid and task data will be deleted from our servers within 30 days.

To request deletion or if you have any questions, please just email me directly at tom@meettoad.co.uk

6. Contact Us

If you have any questions at all about this policy or your data, please don't hesitate to reach out. I'm the founder, and I read every email.
