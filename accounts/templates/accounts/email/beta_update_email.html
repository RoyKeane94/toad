First, I just want to say a huge thank you. The level of engagement, the number of you who are already using Toad regularly, and the quality of the feedback you've sent over the past two weeks has been incredible. It's been so motivating to see.

You spoke, and I listened. My focus has been on implementing your suggestions in a way that stays true to our "purposefully simple" philosophy. Here are the key improvements we've made, all based on your feedback.

The Frills That Matter: What's New in Toad

Our goal isn't to add more features, but to remove friction. These updates are designed to make the core experience of using Toad faster, more intuitive, and more delightful.

✓
A Slicker Mobile Experience:
We've redesigned the mobile view to be a powerful, focused workspace. While desktop gives the best overview, mobile is now much more than just an on-the-go option.
✓
Full Drag-and-Drop:
You can now drag tasks not just within a cell, but across any row or column to instantly re-organise your grid.
✓
Inline Editing:
No more pop-ups. You can now edit the text of any task, row, or column simply by clicking on it.
✓
Grid Groups:
For those of you with a growing number of grids, you can now organise them into simple groups like "Work" and "Personal" on your main dashboard.
✓
Simplified Grid Switching:
We've removed the "Switch Grid" dropdown in favor of a cleaner, more intuitive tab-like system for your pinned grids.
✓
Calendar Reminders:
You can now add a date to any task and, with one click, send it to your personal calendar.
✓
Archive Grids:
Instead of permanently deleting completed tasks, you can now archive them to a new grid, keeping your workspace clean without losing your history.
✓
Save Your Own Templates:
This was a big one. You can now save any grid you've perfected as a reusable template for your own use.

What We Haven't Built (and Why)

Just as important as what we build is what we choose to leave out. A few of you have made excellent suggestions for features like two-way calendar sync, sub-tasks, and notes.

We've decided not to build these, and I want to be transparent about why. Toad's purpose is to be the antidote to the complex, bloated software that creates more work than it saves. Our goal is to provide clarity, not more features.

Instead of adding complexity, we believe the "Toad way" to solve these problems is often simpler:

Toad is not a calendar: Many of you have asked for a full calendar sync. We've decided against this because our goal is to be a simple planner, not a complex scheduling tool. For key deadlines, you can still use the one-way calendar export to send a task to your diary.
For complex projects with sub-tasks: We find it's much clearer to create a new, dedicated grid for that project. Our new grid switching system makes it super easy to switch between grids.
No AI Grid Generation: Some of you suggested using AI to create grids. Our templates are the "thoughtful AI"—clear, proven starting points designed with care to solve your specific problems instantly.
